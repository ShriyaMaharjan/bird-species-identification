The Eurasian Magpie (Pica pica) is a highly adaptable and intelligent bird species that can be found across a vast geographical range, spanning from Europe and Asia to North Africa. These birds are known for their striking black and white plumage and their ability to thrive in diverse environments, including urban areas and forests.

As for its characteristics:
1. **Plumage:** Eurasian Magpies have distinctive black and white plumage, with a glossy sheen on their feathers. Their long tails and rounded wings contribute to their elegant appearance.
2. **Intelligence:** Magpies are renowned for their high level of intelligence and problem-solving abilities. They have been observed using tools, recognizing themselves in mirrors, and displaying complex social behaviors.
3. **Vocalizations:** These birds are quite vocal, with a variety of calls including melodious warbles, harsh chattering, and imitations of other bird species. They use vocalizations for communication within their social groups.
4. **Omnivorous Diet:** Eurasian Magpies have a diverse diet that includes insects, small mammals, seeds, fruits, and even scraps of human food. Their adaptability contributes to their widespread distribution.
5. **Nesting Behavior:** Magpies are known for building large, conspicuous nests high in trees. These nests are often made of twigs and can be quite intricate in design. The birds exhibit strong family bonds and cooperative breeding behaviors.
